Pharmacy

A career I hope to have in the future is a pharmacist. This is because I am interested in helping others within the medical field. A pharmacist is someone who dispenses medications and provides information on the medications to patients. In a hospital setting, a pharmacist would complete orders for medication from other staff in the hospital. They would analyze information on a patient to formulate a dosage of a medication. They also discuss with patients on the effects and concerns of the medication.

Information + Requirements
Salary $132,750 per year $63.82 per hour
Education Doctoral Degree 3-8 years
Skills/Traits Analytical skills: to figure out what a patient should take Communication skills: the job requires talking to patients and others. They must be able to effectively communicate information on a medication. Compassionate: They should be considerate and feel sympathy towards the patient's needs and problems. Detail oriented: a pharmacist should be prescribing an accurate medication for a patient
