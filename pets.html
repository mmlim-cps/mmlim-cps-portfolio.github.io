PETS

Sesame

This is Sesame. He is 3 years old and is pretty fat. He likes to eat dry food and sleep in a spread out position. He likes other people and really enjoys getting pets. He is made to eat, sleep, and be a house cat. He is not fit for the wild. Sesame has a favorite toy sloth. He oftentimes will hold it in his mouth and go around the house meowing. He sometimes does this at night. There is a chance he may be or partially be an alien or even human. This is only speculation, though.

Penny

This is Penny. She is 3 years old and is small but plump. She loves to eat wet food and sleep like an actual cat. She does not like new people, in fact, she hides from them. She is also anti-collar. It is speculated by others that she is batman; we cannot confirm this, but we also cannot deny it.

They don't like each other sometimes.
